Project

Repositório de Solicitadoria

Independent educational legal study repository in Portuguese, with structured lessons, legal sources and academic progression.

Open website Back to projects

Purpose

A structured legal study repository.

Repositório de Solicitadoria organises legal study materials, lessons, summaries, quizzes and official legal sources into a clear academic path.

Status

Online and in active development.

Focus

Portuguese legal education, structured by subjects, lessons and study progression.

Legal position

Educational project only. It is not legal advice and does not replace qualified legal support.

Direction

Clearer lessons, better navigation, stronger legal sources and practical revision tools.

Core features

What the project provides.

Structured lessons

Subjects are organised into lessons, with progression across the academic path.

Legal sources

Official legal references and source links are used to support the material.

Revision tools

Quizzes, summaries and study support pages help with review and consolidation.

Academic progression

The repository tracks subjects, lessons and completion across the wider study plan.
