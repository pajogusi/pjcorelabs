Project

Leis UK para Portugueses

Practical Portuguese-language guide to UK laws and public information, with official sources and clear disclaimers.

Back to projects

Purpose

Clear UK legal information for Portuguese speakers.

Leis UK para Portugueses is designed to explain public legal information from the United Kingdom in Portuguese, using official sources and practical language.

Status

Planned and in preparation.

Focus

UK public information, legal rules and practical explanations for Portuguese-speaking readers.

Sources

The project will prioritise official sources, legislation and public authority guidance.

Legal position

Educational information only. It is not legal advice and does not replace a solicitor, adviser or qualified professional.

Core principles

How the project should work.

Portuguese explanations

Complex public information should be translated into clear Portuguese without distorting the legal meaning.

Official references

Each topic should link directly to the official source or legal rule being explained.

Practical structure

Subjects should be divided into smaller pages, so each rule can be explained carefully and checked properly.

Clear limits

The project must clearly separate educational information from professional legal advice.
